Privacy Policy 🛡️

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Last Updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect 📝

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you book a trip, create an account, subscribe to our newsletter, or contact us for support. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment information (processed securely via our partners)
  • Traveler documentation (Adhar card, Passport, or Visa details)
  • Travel preferences and booking history

2. How We Use Your Information ⚙️

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and confirm your travel bookings
  • Generate invoices and travel receipts
  • Communicate important updates regarding your trips
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send newsletters and promotional offers (if subscribed)

3. Data Protection and Security 🔒

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your traveler documentation (like Adhar or Passport) is used solely for booking verification purposes and is handled according to strict security protocols.

4. Cookies Policy 🍪

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some site functionalities.

5. Third-Party Sharing 🤝

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share necessary data with trusted partners (such as hotels and transport providers) only to facilitate your bookings.

6. Refund Consistency 💰

Please note that our privacy policy aligns with our refund policy. All refund requests are processed based on the terms established at the time of booking. For more details, refer to our Terms & Conditions.

7. Contact Us 📧

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@tripzytours.com.
